Quire allows you to pretty-print your tasks so you can keep a hard copy of all your tasks in your hands.

In Quire, simply go to the project of your choice and then select print. You can hit <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>P</kbd> (or <kbd>âŒ˜</kbd> + <kbd>P</kbd> for Mac) to print.
